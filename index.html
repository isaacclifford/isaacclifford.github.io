Waterloo Satellite Design Team

The mission: Send a cube satellite into space to map Arctic ice depletion using GNSS reflectometry

I represented the Waterloo Satellite Design Team in the 2016 Canadian Satellite Design Challenge in Ottawa. During the challenge, we tested the structural integrity of the cube satellite by putting it on a vibration table and attaching sensors to various parts of the system.

Currently, I am a member of the command and data handling sub-team. I created the initial schematic and PCB board level design of the commander board by modifying an existing design to meet size and functionality requirements. The task included:

  • Learning how to use Altium Circuit Maker
  • Adding and removing components on the schematic
  • Rearranging and remapping components wires on the PCB design
  • Reshaping and resizing the PCB shape
  • Communicating with sub-team leaders about needed components and pin assignments
